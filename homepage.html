ABOUT US

Learning with Urstudymate

With millions of students globally, we are the first of its kind online portal for school children in providing supporting materials for students appearing for the board examinations. We cater to the learning needs of school students of leading educational boards like Cambridge International examinations (IGCSE & AS, A LEVEL), International Baccalaureate (IBDP, CBSE(IX-XII)) and Integrated Curriculum for IIT & NEET Foundation.

At Urstudymate, we believe that the students prepare well for the examinations in terms of concepts but when it comes to solving questions in their examinations ending up with incorrect answers because of lack of practice. We at Urstudymate will provide a suitable topical worksheet which are purely extracted from the past papers aligned according to topic wise with marking scheme, so that the learner will get a deep understanding of the concepts for the successful preparation of the examination.
